About the Art

"Hauntopia is a generative art collection of 10,000 spooky yet cute ghost images created by Immaculate, an artist from India. The collection has been minted as non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and is available for fans of the supernatural to collect and trade on the blockchain. Each NFT in the collection has a unique combination of the 'Yah-Boo' and 'Nah-Boo' characteristics, along with a set of attributes, including the number of 'Yah-Boo' and 'Nah-Boo' ghosts, canvas color, rotation range, and a rank based on the number of rotations and 'Yah-Boo' ghosts in the image.
